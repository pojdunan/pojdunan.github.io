Security Philosophy

I believe security should be built into networks from the ground up, not bolted on as an afterthought. My approach combines:

  • Zero Trust Principles: Verify explicitly, least privilege access, assume breach
  • Defense in Depth: Multiple layers of security controls
  • Continuous Monitoring: Real-time threat detection and response
  • Automation: Consistent enforcement of security policies
